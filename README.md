a# 🍕 Pizza Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project is an interactive **Power BI dashboard** developed to analyze pizza sales performance and uncover actionable business insights. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of revenue trends, customer ordering behavior, and product performance.

The analysis helps stakeholders monitor business growth, identify peak sales periods, and optimize operational decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total sales performance
- Identify top-selling pizzas
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Track monthly revenue trends
- Discover peak sales hours and days
- Evaluate revenue contribution by pizza size

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** 817.86K
- **Total Quantity Sold:** 50K
- **Total Orders:** 21K
- **Average Selling Price:** 16.50

---

## 📈 Visualizations Included

### Revenue by Month
Displays monthly revenue trends to monitor business performance throughout the year.

### Revenue by Size
Shows how different pizza sizes contribute to total revenue.

### Top 5 Pizzas
Highlights the best-performing pizzas based on generated revenue.

### Peak Hours Analysis
Identifies the busiest ordering periods during the day.

### Peak Days Analysis
Shows which days generate the highest customer orders.

### Interactive Category Filter
Allows users to dynamically filter dashboard visuals by pizza category.

## 📂 Dataset Information
The dataset contains:
- Order ID
- Pizza Name
- Category
- Pizza Size
- Quantity
- Revenue
- Order Date
- Order Time

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Large-sized pizzas generated the highest revenue.
- Fridays and Saturdays recorded the highest order volumes.
- Peak sales occurred during lunch and evening periods.
- July and November showed strong revenue performance.
- Chicken pizzas dominated the top-selling products.

---

## ✅ Recommendations
- Increase staffing during peak business hours.
- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products.
- Promote low-selling pizza categories through discounts.
- Optimize inventory planning for large-sized pizzas.
